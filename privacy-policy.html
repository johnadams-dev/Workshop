Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 5, 2026  ·  Last Updated: April 5, 2026
Overview

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Adams, Cameron & Co. ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our real estate agents and staff who use our internal coaching and business planning tools, including the AC Pathfinder coaching program, business plan generator, and related applications accessible through johnadams.org and associated domains.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use our internal tools and services. By using these tools, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

These tools are for internal Adams, Cameron & Co. use only. Access is limited to licensed agents, managers, and staff with valid @adamscameron.com Google Workspace accounts.

Section 1

Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

When using our business planning and coaching tools, we collect information you enter, including:

  • Your name, office location, and contact information
  • Business goals, earning targets, and market focus areas
  • Personal motivations and professional objectives
  • Weekly activity reports and check-in responses
  • Qualitative feedback including challenges faced and opportunities identified
  • Self-assessments of productivity and mood

Information Collected Automatically

  • Your Google Workspace identity (name, email address) when you authenticate
  • Device type and browser information for compatibility purposes
  • Usage patterns within our tools to improve the coaching experience

SMS Messaging Information

If you enroll in our SMS coaching program (AC Pathfinder), we collect:

  • Your mobile phone number (from your agent profile or as provided)
  • Message content from SMS check-in conversations
  • Message delivery status and timestamps
  • Opt-in and opt-out records
Section 2

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To generate personalized business plans tailored to your goals and market
  • To deliver weekly SMS coaching check-ins and activity reminders
  • To track your progress against your stated goals over time
  • To provide your assigned sales manager or coach with insight into your performance and challenges
  • To identify coaching and training opportunities that may help you achieve your goals
  • To schedule and prepare for monthly coaching sessions
  • To improve our coaching tools and programs over time
  • To send calendar invitations for coaching sessions via Google Calendar

We do not sell your personal information to any third party, and we do not use your information for advertising purposes outside of Adams, Cameron & Co.'s internal programs.

Section 3

SMS Messaging — Your Rights

If you enroll in our SMS coaching program, the following terms apply:

Opt-In

You opt in to receive SMS messages by completing the enrollment process in the AC Pathfinder business plan generator. Enrollment is entirely voluntary. You will not receive SMS coaching messages unless you explicitly complete the opt-in process.

Types of Messages

By opting in, you consent to receive the following types of SMS messages from Adams, Cameron & Co. at the phone number you provide:

  • Weekly coaching check-in questions
  • Activity reminders tied to your business plan commitments
  • Playbook task reminders (open house prep, listing appointment prep, showing day prep)
  • Coaching session scheduling confirmations and reminders
  • Brief motivational messages from your coach

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your coaching plan. Typically you will receive 1-3 messages per week during active coaching periods.

Opt-Out

You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. You may also pause your coaching program through the AC Pathfinder web interface or by contacting your sales manager. After opting out, you will receive one confirmation message and no further SMS messages unless you re-enroll.

Message and Data Rates

Standard message and data rates from your mobile carrier may apply to SMS messages you receive and send.

Help

Reply HELP to any message for assistance, or contact us at johnadams@adamscameron.com.

Section 4

Information Sharing

Your information is shared only as follows:

Within Adams, Cameron & Co.

  • Your assigned coach or sales manager — sees your check-in responses, progress scores, and coaching snapshots to support your development
  • Your office coordinator — may have read access to your activity data to provide timely operational support
  • Operations management — Kelly Brooks and John Adams have administrative access for program oversight

Third-Party Service Providers

We use the following third-party services to operate our coaching tools. Each is bound by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Cloud Platform — database hosting and infrastructure (privacy policy)
  • Google Workspace — authentication, email, and calendar integration (privacy policy)
  • Twilio — SMS message delivery (privacy policy)
  • Google Gemini API — AI-powered business plan generation (terms)

We do not share your personal information with any other third parties without your explicit consent, except as required by law.

Section 5

Data Retention

We retain your coaching data for the duration of your affiliation with Adams, Cameron & Co. and for a period of three years following your departure, to support any legitimate business, legal, or compliance needs.

SMS opt-out records are retained indefinitely to honor your preferences.

You may request access to or deletion of your personal data by contacting johnadams@adamscameron.com. Deletion requests will be honored within 30 days, subject to any legal retention obligations.

Section 6

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encrypted database storage on Google Cloud SQL
  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Google OAuth authentication — we never store your Google password
  • Access controls limiting data visibility to appropriate staff roles
  • Regular automated backups with point-in-time recovery

No system is completely secure. If you believe your information has been compromised, please contact us immediately at johnadams@adamscameron.com.

Section 7

Your Rights

As a user of our internal tools, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information in your profile
  • Request deletion of your personal data (subject to retention obligations)
  • Opt out of SMS messaging at any time by replying STOP
  • Withdraw from the coaching program at any time without penalty

To exercise any of these rights, contact johnadams@adamscameron.com.

Section 8

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page and notify active coaching program participants via email. Your continued use of our tools after any update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
