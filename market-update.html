Market Report Generator

Professional MLS Market Analysis in Seconds

The Market Report Generator accepts an export from the MLS (Daytona's Flex or StellarMLS) and shows how the market is performing for that dataset. To use it, run a search with active and sold listings for a particular area — include price and square feet — and export it to a file. Choose that export below, optionally enter your subject property details, and the tool will generate a full market analysis.
